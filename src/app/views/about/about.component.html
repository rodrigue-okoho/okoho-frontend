About OKOHO Consulting UG

OKOHO Consulting UG is committed to fair, transparent and humane international recruitment of skilled workers. We support German companies in the recruitment process of skilled workers and trainees in/from third countries. The following services are offered:

  • Recruitment of skilled workers: We recruit suitable specialists and trainees for your company for certain approved professional groups.
  • Supporting the recruitment process: Unsere Experten aus Drittstaaten überprüfen gründlich die Bewerbungsunterlagen Ihrer Bewerber*innen aus Drittstaaten. Wir helfen Ihnen, ein qualitative Einschätzung der vorliegenden Bewerbungsunterlagen zu haben. Auf Wunsch führen wir für Sie erste Bewerbungsgespräche auf der Muttersprache Ihrer Bewerber*innen, so dass ihre wahren Motivationen ohne sprachliche Barriere in Erfahrung gebracht werden können.
  • Immigration accompaniment: We take care of all administrative procedures in the immigration process for you, coordinate the visa application and prepare both your future specialist/trainee and your team for good, success-oriented and constructive cooperation.
  • Relocation support: We support your specialist/trainee in preparing for their trip and looking for accommodation in Germany. We pick up your specialist/trainee from the airport and gradually introduce them to German society.
  • Promoting integration: After the skilled worker/trainee arrives in Germany, we support your skilled worker/trainee right up to the final administrative process as well as in networking with other people and with our holistic integration concept, your skilled worker/trainee can more easily gain a foothold in this country. We remain the contact person for your specialist/trainee for questions about everyday life in Germany.
